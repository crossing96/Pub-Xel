# Importing nbib Files

**Role:** Turn a PubMed **nbib** export into a filled Excel worksheet or TSV file.

---

## How

1. **File → Open** (or drag the `.nbib` file onto Pub-Xel).
2. Choose **Excel** or **TSV**.
3. Save where you want.

Pub-Xel fills in article info the same way as **Import PubMed Data**.

Useful when you export many articles at once from PubMed.

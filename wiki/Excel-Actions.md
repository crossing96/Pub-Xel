# Excel Actions

These buttons work on **whatever cells you have currently selected in your Excel worksheet**. Keep Excel open, and select your PMID cells. Do not enter cell edit mode. 

While Pub-Xel is working, don’t click in Excel until it finishes.

---

## Import PubMed Data

**Role:** Pull article info from PubMed into your worksheet.

1. Select cells with PMIDs (the **Ref** column).
2. Click **Import PubMed Data**.

Title, journal, abstract, citation, and other enabled columns are filled in for you.

---

## Check if Files Exist

**Role:** Show which PMIDs already have a PDF in your library.

1. Select PMID cells.
2. Click **Check if Files Exist**.

Cells with a matching **PMID.pdf** (e.g., `33301246.pdf`) in Main library turn **blue**.

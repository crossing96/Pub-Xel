# Clipboard Actions

Copy article IDs (PMIDs) with **Ctrl+C**, then use Pub-Xel to open PDFs or inspect them.

You can copy from Excel, Word, PubMed, or anywhere you see PMIDs or PubMed links.

---

## Open Files

**Role:** Open PDFs that match the IDs you copied.

Copy IDs → click **Open Files** or press **Alt+Shift+K**.

Pub-Xel looks in your **Main library** (and secondary folders if you set them) for **PMID.pdf** files (e.g., `33301246.pdf`).

---

## Inspect Files

**Role:** Open a window to review your IDs, see PubMed info, open or export PDFs, or make a new worksheet.

Copy IDs → click **Inspect Files** or press **Alt+Shift+J**.

See [Inspect Window](Inspect-Window.md).

---

## View PubMed (hotkey)

**Role:** Open PubMed in your browser for copied PMIDs.

Press **Alt+Shift+P**.

---

Copying **files** directly from File Explorer is also supported.

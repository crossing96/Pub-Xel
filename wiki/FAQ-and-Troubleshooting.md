# FAQ

---

## Is Pub-Xel on Mac?

**Not yet.** Windows only for now.

---

## Why doesn’t closing the window quit Pub-Xel?

It usually goes to the **system tray** (near the clock). Right-click the Pub-Xel icon → **Exit** to quit. You can turn this off in **Preferences → Other**.

---

## Excel actions don’t work

- Open Excel and your worksheet first.
- Select the PMID cells you want.
- Wait until Pub-Xel finishes — don’t click in Excel during import.

---

## Check if Files Exist — nothing turned blue

- Save PDFs in **Main library** as **PMID.pdf** (e.g., `33301246.pdf`).
- Check the folder path in **Preferences → Library**.

---

## Hotkey does nothing

Save Preferences, **restart** Pub-Xel, and make sure it’s still running (tray icon). Did you copy IDs first (**Ctrl+C**)?

---

## “Clipboard is empty”

Copy PMID numbers or PubMed links as **text** (**Ctrl+C**), not a screenshot.

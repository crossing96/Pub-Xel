# Installation

## Download

1. Go to [https://pubxel.org/download/latest](https://pubxel.org/download/latest/).
2. Download the **Windows** installer.
3. Run it and follow the prompts.

> **macOS is not available yet.**

---

## First launch

- A **Welcome** screen walks you through the basics.
- You’ll be asked to save your first **Pub-Xel Worksheet** (Excel file).
- Closing the window may keep Pub-Xel running in the **system tray** (near the clock). That’s normal.
